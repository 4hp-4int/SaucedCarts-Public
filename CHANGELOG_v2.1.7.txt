SaucedCarts v2.1.7
Released: 2026-05-22
====================

A transfer-correctness release. Vehicle trunks now talk to carts both
ways, stacked containers stop dropping items in the wrong box, shelves
and fridges refresh their look after a transfer, and moving a stack of
nails no longer crawls one item at a time. Plus a multiplayer dupe fix
and some corpse-storage BETA polish.


BUG FIXES
---------

* Vehicle trunk <-> cart transfers didn't work — the action played
  but nothing moved. Now works in both directions, for trunks,
  gloveboxes, seats, and trailer beds, whether you're sitting in the
  vehicle or standing next to it.

* Two same-type containers stacked on one tile (two crates, a fridge
  + freezer, a double-door wardrobe) put the item in the WRONG box —
  you'd drag to the top one and it landed in the bottom. Now lands in
  the box you actually clicked.

* Shelves, fridges, and stacked crates didn't visually update their
  full/empty appearance after a cart transfer. They now refresh the
  same way they do for a normal vanilla transfer.

* Moving a stack of light items (nails, screws, etc.) to or from a
  cart played one slow transfer per item. Same-type light stacks now
  bundle into a single action, and transfer time scales with item
  weight just like vanilla — a nail is quick, a sledgehammer takes a
  moment.

* Right-click "Unequip" on a cart could duplicate it on dedicated
  multiplayer servers. Fixed.

* Hardened the server-side transfer handler against malformed network
  input so a misbehaving client can't interrupt a transfer.


CORPSE STORAGE (BETA)
---------------------

Still opt-in and off by default — toggle "Enable Corpse Storage
[BETA]" in sandbox. This release polishes it:

* The BETA could silently switch itself ON when you loaded a save
  made before the feature existed. It now stays OFF unless you
  explicitly enable it.

* Fixed a "corpse appears, then instantly vanishes" flicker when
  unloading a body that was close to its decay threshold. It now
  matches vanilla's despawn point exactly.

* If your cart is full (or missing) when you try to load a corpse,
  you now get an immediate on-screen message instead of a silent
  no-op — including in multiplayer race situations.

* Removed the experimental "cart corpse stink" option. It couldn't be
  made to work reliably in multiplayer, so the sandbox toggle is gone.


COMPATIBILITY
-------------

Save-safe. No save-data changes — safe to upgrade mid-save. The old
"corpse stink" sandbox option is simply ignored if your config still
has it. Carts created on older versions keep working.
